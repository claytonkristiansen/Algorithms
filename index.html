Grid Painter

  • Press B or S while hovering over an intersection to select begin/start point
  • Press E or F while hovering over an intersection to select end/finish point
  • Left Click on an intersection to add/remove obstacle
  • Left Click and drag to leave a trail of obstacles
  • Hole R and Left Click and drag to erase
  • Press Esc to clear the grid
  • Press ENTER to run Dikstra's and find the shortest path
